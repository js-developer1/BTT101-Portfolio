What is E-Waste and how does it affect us?

E-Waste or Electronic Waste is a form of waste that is comprised up of technology that is no loger used for example, old smart phones, old tv's and more.



Mission of Computing Inc.

Our mission is to educate people about electronic waste, its affects on humans, and what to do when youneed to throw out electronics. Every electronic item that is thrown away in the wrong bin will be taken to other countries to deal with the garbage. Eventually the toxic chemicals within those electronics will come back to use as the chemicals found within these electronics. That is why it is super important to know how to throw away unusable electronics. That's why we partnered up with EPRA to help solve this issue. You can go to their website at https://recyclemyelectronics.ca.
